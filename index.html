Computational Omics Lab

Biomedical Science x Data Science

The COmics Lab is led by Shimin Shuai, PhD, and is a part of the Department of Human Cell Biology and Genetics, School of Medicine, Southern University of Science and Technology (SUSTech) at Shenzhen, China.

Our mission is to advance human health by decoding disease ecosystems with computational omics.

To learn more about our research, please see our projects and publications. Also, don’t forget to meet our team and check out current job opportunities!
