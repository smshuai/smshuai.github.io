My name is Shimin Shuai (帅世民). I was asked many times about what's the meaning of my name in Chinese.
My family name (Shuai/帅) simply means *handsome* in Chinese; my first name is identical to a famous Chinese ruler, the [Emperor Taizong of Tang](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Emperor_Taizong_of_Tang), although this is a sheer coincidence as the new born registration officer misspelled my name from *Shi-ming* to *Shi-min* -- a well-known behavior with southern Mandarin accent. It turns out that there may be another coincidence, debatably, Taizong and I also share the same birthday.
